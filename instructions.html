📖 How to Play

Master the 7-round investment challenge

🎯 The Challenge

You start with $10,000 and face 7 hidden years of market history.

Your goal: maximize your final capital by allocating across 9 asset classes each round.

💡 Key Insight: You must allocate your capital before knowing that year's returns, simulating real-world investment decisions.

🔄 How Each Round Works

1
View your capital — you start with $10,000 and reinvest your returns each round.
2
Choose your input mode — you can enter allocations as dollar amounts, percentages, or both.
3
Allocate your money — enter amounts for each of the 9 asset classes.
4
Track your remaining capital — the interface shows how much you have left to allocate.
5
Submit your allocation — click "Done — reveal returns" to see the hidden year's performance.
6
Review your results — see your return amount, percentage, and new capital.
7
Repeat — your new capital is reinvested into the next round.

📊 Input Modes

Choose how you want to enter your allocations:

💰 Dollar Amount 📈 Percentage (%) 🔄 Both
  • 💰 Dollar Amount — enter exact dollar amounts for each asset
  • 📈 Percentage — enter percentages that must sum to 100%
  • 🔄 Both — enter either dollar amounts OR percentages, and the other will auto-calculate

⚡ Quick Actions

Speed up your allocation with these preset buttons:

⚖️ Equal Split Allocates evenly across all 9 assets (~11.1% each)
💵 All Cash Puts 100% of your capital into Cash
📈 Equity Heavy 70% Equities, 30% Fixed Income
🛡️ Defensive 30% Equities, 70% Fixed Income
🗑️ Clear All Resets all allocations to zero

📋 Tracking Your Performance

  • 📊 Results page — shows your performance for each completed round
  • 📈 Portfolio page — tracks all your allocations, returns, and capital across rounds
  • 📋 History log — on the main game page, you can see all your previous rounds
  • 💭 Reflection area — use the note space after each round to document what you learned

🔄 Resetting the Game

If you want to start over, click the "Reset Entire Game" button at the bottom of the game page.

⚠️ Warning: This will delete all your progress, history, and start you back at Round 1 with $10,000. This action cannot be undone!
